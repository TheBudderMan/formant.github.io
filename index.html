    A little about myself

    Stagma Records // Intaface Audio // Boey Audio
    Tyler Johnston, AKA Formant is a new,
    up and coming drum and bass
    artist from Calgary, Alberta.
    DJing from an early age he
    has been rocking the sounds
    of jump-up, tech-step and nuerofunk
    from his earlier years in Winnipegs
    electronic scene,
    playing weekly at AM afterhours
    under the name, Spam as well
    as at Soundwave Entertainment
    events such as Tranceformers,
    Scream and Vinyl Fantasy IX.
    Now having his own music released on
    Canadian label Intaface Audio,
    Irish labels Stagma Records and Boey Audio,
    he now does so with his own productions in drum and bass.
